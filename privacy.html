Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Welcome to vgncount.com. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data We Collect

When you join the Global Vegan Census, we collect only your email address and an approximate geographic location (derived via IP address) solely to display anonymous aggregated data on our global map.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

Your email is used strictly for sending a single verification link to confirm your participation. We do not use your email for marketing, spam, or share it with third parties.

3. Data Security

Data is securely stored via private Google infrastructure protected against unauthorized access. You can request the complete deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your information whenever you wish.
